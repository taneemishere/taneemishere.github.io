Deep Image In-Panting: Generative Vs. Recurrent Models

Research Project (Personal)

Taneem Ullah Jan*

*First Author
Code Demo Paper



Description

Proposed a Row Flattened LSTM from PixelCNNs to show a simpler model can achieve good results. This is was carried out by comparative testing of encoders and discriminators based on top of state-of-the-art models against basic CNN architectures. And also due to improvement of the context encoders by executing several major training tricks on GAN and remodel the network to WGAN.
