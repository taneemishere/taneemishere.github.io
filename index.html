about me

I am working as Research AI Engineer at BHuman Technologies Inc., where I am trying to apply my research on real-world and challenging projects, and contributing to the advancement in the field of computer vision and neural head and body avatars. Prior to this, I graduated from University of Engineering and Technology Peshawar, where I pursued my passion for computer science and machine learning. My focus studies was on deep neural networks and generative models. I did my research work and thesis in the same at one of the AI Labs under the supervision of Dr. Zakira Inayat. My goal is to make machines able to learn, see and generate with intelligent algorithms and predictive modelling. I am constantly seeking new research opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in this field.

Research Interests:
Interested in formulating, developing, and advancing intelligent computer vision systems, specifically harnessing neural rendering methods for diverse uses such as enhancing video synthesis and digital human representations.
