About Me

I am a graduate student from University of Engineering and Technology Peshawar, where I pursued my passion for computer science and machine learning. My focus studies and were on deep neural networks and data generative models and I did my research work in one of the AI Labs under the supervision of Dr. Zakira Inayat. My goal is to make machines able to learn, see and write with intelligent algorithms, and I am constantly seeking new research opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in this field.
